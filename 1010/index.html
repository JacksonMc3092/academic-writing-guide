How to Use This Guide

Each chapter teaches one writing move, connects that move to ENG 1010 assignments, and ends with a Scholar's Blue Book Journal prompt. Use the chapters in order, or return to the chapter that matches the writing problem in front of you.

Chapter Navigation

All chapters use the same mobile-friendly layout, sidebar navigation, quick links, dark mode, print support, and Blue Book Journal structure.
